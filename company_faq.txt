NimbusCart Customer Support FAQ
================================

Welcome to the NimbusCart Help Center. NimbusCart is an online retail platform
offering electronics, home goods, and lifestyle products. Below you will find
answers to the most common questions we receive from our customers, organized
by topic.

REFUND POLICY
-------------
NimbusCart offers a 30-day return and refund window starting from the date of
delivery. To be eligible for a refund, items must be unused, in their original
packaging, and accompanied by the original invoice or order confirmation
email. Once we receive and inspect the returned item, refunds are processed
within 5 to 7 business days back to the original payment method.

Refunds for items paid via credit or debit card typically appear on the
customer's statement within 2 additional billing cycles, depending on the
bank. Refunds for orders paid through NimbusCart Wallet are instant and
credited back to the wallet balance immediately after inspection is complete.

Certain categories are non-refundable, including perishable goods, gift
cards, downloadable software, and items marked "Final Sale" at checkout. If a
product arrives damaged or defective, customers should report the issue
within 48 hours of delivery to qualify for a free replacement or full refund,
including any shipping charges paid.

Gold members receive an extended 45-day return window instead of the standard
30 days. Platinum members receive a 60-day extended return window and are
eligible for reverse pickup at no additional cost, meaning a courier will
collect the item directly from their address.

DELIVERY TIMELINES
-------------------
Standard delivery across most metro areas takes 3 to 5 business days from the
date of order confirmation. Non-metro and rural areas may experience delivery
times of 5 to 8 business days depending on courier availability in that
region. Delivery timelines may be extended during major sale events, national
holidays, or due to unforeseen weather disruptions.

NimbusCart offers three delivery speeds at checkout: Standard (3-5 business
days), Express (1-2 business days, available in select metro pin codes), and
Scheduled Delivery, which allows customers to choose a specific date and time
slot for an additional fee.

Silver members receive free Standard delivery on all orders above 500 rupees.
Gold members receive free Express delivery on all orders regardless of order
value. Platinum members receive free Express delivery with no minimum order
value and get priority dispatch, meaning their orders are picked and packed
ahead of standard queue orders.

Customers can track their shipment in real time from the "My Orders" section
of their account or via the tracking link sent by email and SMS once the
order has been dispatched from the warehouse.

MEMBERSHIP BENEFITS
--------------------
NimbusCart offers three membership tiers: Silver, Gold, and Platinum.
Membership is free to join and tiers are automatically upgraded based on
annual spend, though customers may also purchase a tier directly.

Silver members receive free standard shipping on orders above 500 rupees, a
5 percent discount on their birthday month, and early access to sale events
by 2 hours.

Gold members receive all Silver benefits plus free Express delivery, a 10
percent discount on their birthday month, extended 45-day returns, and early
access to sale events by 12 hours. Gold members also receive a dedicated
customer support queue with reduced wait times.

Platinum members receive all Gold benefits plus a 15 percent discount on
their birthday month, a 60-day extended return window, free reverse pickup
for returns, priority order dispatch, and access to 24/7 premium customer
support with a dedicated relationship manager for orders above 10,000 rupees.

Membership tier is reviewed every 12 months based on total spend during that
period. Customers can view their current tier and progress toward the next
tier in the "Membership" section of their account dashboard.

ACCOUNT CANCELLATION
----------------------
Customers who wish to cancel or permanently delete their NimbusCart account
can do so from Account Settings by selecting "Close My Account." Before
cancellation is processed, any pending orders must be delivered or cancelled,
and any outstanding wallet balance must be withdrawn or used, since wallet
balances cannot be refunded after account closure.

Once submitted, account cancellation requests are processed within 7 business
days. During this period, the account will be temporarily deactivated and
inaccessible. After the 7-day window, all personal data is permanently
deleted from active systems in accordance with our data retention policy,
though anonymized transaction records may be retained for legal and tax
compliance purposes.

Customers with an active Platinum membership subscription must cancel their
membership subscription separately before closing their account, as
membership fees are non-refundable once the current billing cycle has begun.

If a customer changes their mind during the 7-day processing window, they can
contact customer support to halt the cancellation and restore full account
access.

SUPPORT CONTACT HOURS
------------------------
Standard customer support is available via chat and email from 9:00 AM to
9:00 PM, Monday through Saturday, excluding public holidays. Email queries
submitted outside these hours are queued and answered on the next business
day. Average response time for email support is under 24 hours.

Phone support is available from 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday through Friday,
for order-related escalations only.

PREMIUM CUSTOMER SUPPORT RULES
---------------------------------
Premium customer support is available exclusively to Gold and Platinum
members. Gold members are routed to a dedicated support queue during standard
support hours (9:00 AM to 9:00 PM, Monday through Saturday) with an average
response time under 10 minutes on chat.

Platinum members receive 24/7 premium customer support, available every day
of the week including public holidays, through chat, email, and phone. Each
Platinum member is assigned a dedicated relationship manager if their
cumulative annual spend exceeds 10,000 rupees. This relationship manager can
be reached directly for order issues, refund escalations, and personalized
shopping assistance.

Silver members and non-members use the standard support queue and do not
have access to the dedicated Gold queue or the 24/7 Platinum support line.
Any customer, regardless of tier, can escalate an unresolved issue by
requesting a "supervisor callback," which is honored within 48 hours.
